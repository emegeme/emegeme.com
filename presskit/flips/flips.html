Description

Prepare your Mind for the latest Cards-based Mind-Training. Try to find PAIRS and TRIOS or just try to Repeat a Cards Sequence in SIMON game. Three funny Games to keep your Mind busy while contemplating an Awesome design... Unlock and Collect the 64 exclusive vegan Cards!

GAME FEATURES:

- 5 Game Modes

- 64 Levels to Play with Increasing Difficulty

- 64 Unique Hand-Drawn Cards to Unlock and Collect

- Awesome Design with Extreme Care for Detail

- Hours of Fun and Mind Training

- 8 Languages: English, Spanish, French, Italian, German, Russian, Japanese, Simplified Chinese
